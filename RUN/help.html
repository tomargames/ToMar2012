Runes Help


Time does not matter at all in Runes.
Click on a square to place the Rune.
Place the Rune adjacent to a rune that matches in type or color.
When a row or column is completed, you get a bonus and the squares will turn gray.
Complete the level by turning all the squares gray.
Click on the WasteCan to discard a Rune.
The WasteCan will hold up to 3 Runes; if you try to discard when it's full, you lose.
Each time you place a Rune, it subtracts one from the WasteCan.
You get fewer bombs and wild cards as the levels progress.
A bomb will erase any rune on the board, or it can be discarded.
Scoring is as follows:
  • If a Rune is placed touching one other Rune, you get 5 points multiplied by the level.
  • If a Rune is placed touching two other Runes, you get 25 points multiplied by the level.
  • If a Rune is placed touching three other Rune, you get 125 points multiplied by the level.
  • If a Rune is placed touching four other Rune, you get 625 points multiplied by the level.
  • Completing a row or a column gets you 1000 points multiplied by the level.
  • Completing a level gets you 10,000 points multiplied by the level.
Special things that happen:
  • At Level 10, you'll get an additional Rune.
  • At Level 20, you'll get an additional color.
  • At Level 30, the WasteCan will shrink to holding only 2 Runes.
  • At Level 40, the WasteCan will shrink to holding only 1 Rune.
The top score for any ToMar game is 2147483647. If you get to that score, the game will end, but no one will ever beat you...
