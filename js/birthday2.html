What Your Birthday Number Means

#1 - The Originator
You are an original. Coming up with new ideas and executing them is natural to you. Having things your own way also seems natural, but may get you labeled as stubborn and arrogant. You are extremely honest, so you may have to work extra hard on diplomacy skills. You like to take the initiative and are probably a leader or a boss, as you like to be the best. Being self-employed is a good choice for you. Lesson to learn: Others' ideas might be just as good or better than your own, so stay open-minded.

Famous #1's:
Tom Hanks, Robert Redford, Hulk Hogan, Carol Burnett, Wynonna Judd, Nancy Reagan, Raquel Welch

#2 - The Peacemaker
You are a born diplomat. You are aware of others' needs and moods, and often think of others before yourself. Naturally analytical and intuitive, you don't like to be alone. Friendship and companionship are very important to you, and can lead you to be successful in life, but you would rather be alone than in an uncomfortable relationship. Being naturally shy, you should learn to boost your self-esteem and express yourself freely. Don't put things off; seize the moment.

Famous #2's:
President Bill Clinton, Madonna, Whoopie Goldberg, Thomas Edison, Wofgang Amadeus Mozart

#3 - The Life of the Party
You are an idealist. You are very creative, social, charming, romantic, and easygoing. You start many things, but don't always see them through. You like others to be happy, and will go to great lengths to keep them that way. You are very popular and idealistic. You may need to learn to see the world from a more realistic point of view.

Famous #3's:
Alan Alda, Ann Landers, Bill Cosby, Melanie Griffith, Salvador Dali, Jodie Foster

#4 - The Conservative
You are sensible and traditional. You like order and routine. You only act when you fully understand what you are expected to do. You like getting your hands dirty and working hard. You are attracted to the outdoors, and you feel an affinity with nature. You are prepared to wait for the things you want; you could be considered to be stubborn and persistent. One thing you could work on is your flexibility (or lack of it), and you should always remember to be nice to yourself.

Famous #4's:
Neil Diamond, Margaret Thatcher, Arnold Schwarzenegger, Tina Turner, Paul Hogan, Oprah Winfrey

#5 - The Non-Conformist
You are an explorer. Your natural curiosity, risk taking, and enthusiasm often land you in hot water. You need diversity; you don't like to be stuck in a rut. The whole world is your school, and you see a learning possibility in every situation. Your questions never stop. You should learn to look before you take action, and make sure you have all the facts before you jump to conclusions.

Famous #5's:
Abraham Lincoln, Charlotte Bronte, Jessica Walter, Vincent van Gogh, Bette Midler, Helen Keller

#6 - The Romantic
You are idealistic, and you need to feel useful in order to be happy. A strong family connection is important to you. Your emotions often influence your decisions. You have a strong urge to take care of and help others. You are very loyal; you take friendship very seriously. You like art and/or music, and would probably make a good teacher. You need to work on your ability to distinguish between the things you can and cannot change.

Famous #6's:
Albert Einstein, Jane Seymour, John Denver, Meryl Streep, Christopher Columbus, Goldie Hawn

#7 - The Intellectual
You are a searcher. Always probing for hidden information, you find it difficult to accept things at face value. Emotions don't sway your decisions. Questioning everything in life, you don't like to be questioned yourself. You're never off to a fast start; your motto is 'slow and steady wins the race'. You come across as being very knowledgeable and somewhat of a philosopher, and maybe somewhat of a loner. You are technically inclined, and you love to uncover secrets. That combination makes you a great researcher. Your only problem is that you live in your own world. You need to learn what is acceptable in the world at large.

Famous #7's:
William Shakespeare, Lucille Ball, Michael Jackson, Joan Baez, Princess Diana

#8 - The Big Shot
You are a problem solver. You are professional, blunt, and to the point. You have good judgement and are very decisive. You have grandiose plans, and you like to live the good life. You are a 'take-charge' person. You view people objectively, but you let them know in no uncertain terms that you are the boss. The thing you need to work on is basing your decisions on your own needs rather than the needs of others.

Famous #8's:
Barbra Streisand, George Harrison, Jane Fonda, Pablo Picasso, Aretha Franklin, Nostradamus

#9 - The Performer
You are a natural entertainer. You are very caring and generous, giving away your last dollar to help someone. With your charm, you have no problem making friends; no one is a stranger to you. You have so many different personalities that people around you have a hard time understanding you sometimes. You are like a chameleon, ever changing and blending in. You have tremendous luck, but can also suffer from extremes in fortune and mood. To be successful, you will need to build a loving foundation.

Famous #9's:
Albert Schweitzer, Shirley MacLaine, Harrison Ford, Gloria Steinem, Jimmy Carter, Elvis Presley
