Your Birthday Number

Your birthdate describes who you are, what you are good at, and what your inborn abilities are. It also points to what you have to learn, and the challenges you are facing.

Your birthday number is calculated by adding the numbers of your birthday over and over again, until only one digit is left. For example, if you were born on February 15, 1956, it would be 2 + 15 + 1956 = 1973, then 1 + 9 + 7 + 3 = 20, then 2 + 0 = 2. So your birthday number would be 2.

Your birthday number will never prevent you from being anything you want, it will just color your choices differently.
