Things to Know
All puzzles can be solved.
Giving up loses 25 points.
You get at least 10 points for solving a puzzle.
Don't use the enter key - use the buttons.
Don't put spaces in your answer - just symbols and numbers.
Numbers can be in any order, as long as the equation works.
